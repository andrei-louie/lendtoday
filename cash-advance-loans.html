Frequently Asked Questions

Looking for more information on how cash advance loans work? Keep reading to get answers to frequently asked questions!
Q: What’s the difference between a cash advance and a payday loan?
A: Very good question, and the answer is very simple…there is no difference except for the name. Both online loans offer funding based on assuming you’ll get paid from your income source (job, disability check, etc) in a couple weeks or “payday.” The money is a “cash advance” on this income and will most often be due back to the lender on your payday.
Q: I’ve heard the interest is high, is that right?
A: Interest and fees are typically more than you would expect with a traditional personal loan at your bank. However, this increased cost is due to the risk the short term lenders take on you. For example, they often offer loans to those with no credit, bad credit or poor employment history. Because of this, banks often are not an option.
Additionally, our direct lenders get you the money you need FAST, while banks could take days, weeks or months to pay out. Exact interest rates and fees will be provided to you BEFORE you agree to the loan, so you’ll always be informed before making a decision.
Q: How long can I borrow the money for?
A: With a cash advance loan, typically the funds are borrowed for a period of 1-week up to 1 month. In most instances funds are borrowed only until your next payday, at which time repayment in full (including any fees and interest) is due.
Q: What do I need to qualify?
A: Every direct lender has their own set of internal criteria for actual “approval.” The best way to determine if you can get approved is to fill out a short application online.
However, some general rules apply, and you normally need the following:
  • Government-issued ID (state ID, passport, drivers license)
  • Some form of income (job/work, or other source)
  • Active checking account in your name
  • Meet the minimum age requirement of 18+
  • Be a US citizen or permanent/legal resident
Q: What happens if I can't repay my loan on time?
A: Speedy Cash recommends that you only borrow what you can afford to pay back. However, we understand that emergencies happen. If you are unable to make your scheduled loan payments, give us a call or stop by one of our stores. We may be able to extend your due date or work out an alternate payment plan.
