How do Online Loans from LendToday Direct Lenders Work?

Simple, just follow the steps below

  • 1. Apply Online
    Our application forms are safe, secure and easy to complete. In a few minutes or less you can be on your way to getting the cash loans you need.
  • 2. Immediate Lending Decisions
    After submitting your application we send your information to our network of direct lenders for review, and you’ll know within minutes if you were approved.
  • 3. Verify Loan Amount & Review Terms
    Once you’re approved, you’ll have an opportunity to review the terms and conditions BEFORE signing for your loan. It is important that you read all the terms, especially the interest rates, fees and payback terms.
  • 4. Sign your loan documents
    Our lenders know you are busy and in need of cash fast! That’s why in most cases you can simply sign electronically from your smartphone, PC or tablet. In some cases faxing may be required.
  • 5. Get your money speedy fast!
    We know you need cash now. That’s why our direct lenders work fast to get you the money you need as quickly as possible. Typically as soon as the next business day you’ll have the funds deposited directly into your bank account.
  • 6. Repay your loan
    Repayment is easy! Who has time to write out checks, deposit funds and mail payments? Well, you won’t have to worry about that. Most of our online cash lenders use pre-authorized repayment meaning that the funds you owe (including any interest and fees) will be directly deducted from your bank account on the due date.
