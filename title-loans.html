How Online Installment Loans Near me Work

Quick. Easy. And best of all…FAST!

  • 1. Fill Out an Application
    Your application process is QUICK, taking less than a few minutes to complete on a smartphone, tablet or PC.
  • 2. Instant Lending Decisions
    Within mere minutes you’ll be informed of your approval or denial, no matter if you submit your application at 2am or 2pm. 24/7 we’re there to make sure you get the help you need.
  • 3. Review Loan Documents
    If approved, you’ll get access to read, save and print all the loan terms, rates and documents to review before agreeing to the loan. Make sure to read these over.
  • 4. Sign your loan documents
    In most cases our direct lenders let you electronically sign your loan documents from the comfort of your home. So as long as you have an internet connection and access to their website a fax is not normally needed (though in some cases it may be required).
  • 5. Access to Funds
    The most important part is getting access to the cash you need when you need it most. Our direct lenders normally deposit funds into your bank account the next business day. Once the bank posts the amount as “available” in your account you are free to withdraw or spend it as needed.
  • 6. Repayment
    Nobody likes to think of this until they have to, but it’s important we cover it. In most cases loan payments are automatically processed from your bank account on the scheduled due dates (if you sign an authorization form). Otherwise, you’ll be required to make payments online directly to the lender with a debit/credit card on the due dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Helping you understand how installment loans work is important to us. That’s why we provide you with both a FAQ and a Learning Center to help educate you on your options and make sure you are an informed consumer.
Read on for just a few of the most common questions customers have:
Q: How much can I borrow?
A: Unlike payday loans, installment loans for bad credit (or no / good credit) normally offer larger sums for borrowing. Those seeking funding can get approved for up to $5000. However, the actual amount you can qualify for may vary based on individual requirements and standards at each lender.
Q: When will I have to pay back my online installment loan ?
A: Unlike cash advance loans, installment loans no credit check are paid back over time in equal sums. For example, if you owe $1000 over 10 months, the repayment would be $100 per month plus any interest and fees that may be associated with the loan. In this way, no credit check installment loans offer a more manageable way to pay back over time.
Q: What are the requirements to apply?
A: While requirements to apply for an online installment loan vary per lender, the following are the most common requirements:
  • Have a valid ID such as a driver’s license or state ID
  • Have a consistent form of income
  • Have a valid checking account in your name
  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be a US citizen or permanent resident
Q: When will I receive funds?
A: If you are approved for an installment loan online then you can expect (in most cases) to get access to money deposited directly to your bank account the next business day. Specific terms will be provided to you after your application before you sign/agree to the loan.
  • Government-issued ID
  • Steady source of income
Q: What happens if I can't repay my loan on time?
A: Speedy Cash recommends that you only borrow what you can afford to pay back. However, we understand that emergencies happen. If you are unable to make your scheduled loan payments, give us a call or stop by one of our stores. We may be able to extend your due date or work out an alternate payment plan.
